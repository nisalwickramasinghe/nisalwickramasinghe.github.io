02 · Research

Four years of
field & lab research

🧫
Oct 2025 – May 2026 · Final Year Project

In Vitro Establishment of Underutilized Fruit Crops in Sri Lanka

Optimized surface sterilization protocols for tissue culture of Lovi, Lawulu, and Ambarella. Evaluated 21 treatment combinations across 150+ explants — achieving 0% contamination with the T21 protocol (fungicide pre-treatment + 50% Clorox + smaller explant size). Statistical analysis via Kruskal-Wallis, ANOVA, and Tukey HSD.

Tissue Culture SPSS ANOVA Conservation
🦠
May 2024 – Jan 2025

Salt Marsh Microorganisms for Sustainable Rice Cultivation

Isolated and characterized microorganisms from Kalpitiya salt marshes to evaluate their plant growth-promoting potential under saline stress. Performed bioassays assessing phytohormone production and phosphate solubilization — exploring sustainable solutions for saline-affected farmland.

Microbiology Saline Stress Bioassay Kalpitiya
🌵
Jan – Mar 2024

CAM Metabolism Study in Kalanchoe pinnata (Akkapana)

Investigated Crassulacean Acid Metabolism (CAM) in K. pinnata by measuring diurnal acid fluctuations through titrations and pH assays. Demonstrated characteristic CAM photosynthetic patterns, deepening understanding of drought-adapted plant physiology.

Plant Physiology CAM Biochemistry Titration
🐦
Dec 2022 – Mar 2023

Behavioral Study of Yellow Wagtail in a Wetland Ecosystem

Conducted systematic field observations of Yellow Wagtail (Motacilla flava) behavioral patterns in a wetland habitat. Documented foraging, movement, and social behaviors — contributing to local biodiversity monitoring and wetland ecology data.

Field Ecology Ethology Biodiversity Wetlands
