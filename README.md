## Research Projects

### 1. In Vitro Establishment of Underutilized Fruit Crops (Final Year Project)
**University of Colombo | 2025–2026**

Developed and optimized surface sterilization protocols for tissue culture of underutilized fruit crops. Evaluated 21 treatment combinations varying explant size, fungicide pre-treatment, and Clorox concentration across 150+ explants.

- Conducted contamination analysis (fungal, bacterial, total) across all treatments
- Applied statistical methods: Kruskal-Wallis, one-way ANOVA, and Tukey HSD post-hoc tests
- Key finding: T21 (smaller explant + fungicide pre-treatment + 50% Clorox) achieved 0% contamination — the optimal protocol
- Tools: SPSS, Excel
